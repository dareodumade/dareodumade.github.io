A LEADING SUPPLIER OF HIGH QUALIY ECO FRIENDLY CHARCOAL PRODUCTS

Mametan Resources Limited is an entity focused on the production, distribution and trade of charcoal and charcoal briquettes with a trade capacity in excess of 30,000MT of charcoal products annually. The company through its parent and experienced management has over the last 15 years developed competitive experience in operating in developing countries.
